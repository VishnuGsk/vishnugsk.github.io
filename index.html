About Us

strong growth in today's rapidly changing healthcare

Our Business



Every day, we strive to market new medicines that are
designed to improve the health and quality of life of
patients around the world - medicines which are
innovative, effective and which offer added benefits
such as reduced side effects or better ways of
functioning.
Boris health care is an India based pharma and health
care organization. The company is marketing
pharmaceutical and healthcare sector products
Boris Health care promoting and marketing their
products in India. The company launched their
ovulation kit and pregnancy kit in Sultanate of Oman.
We also initiated the launch of promising new products
in the year 2020

Our Vision

Quality is the governing factor in all of our
endeavors. Our goal is to know the exact need of our
customers and provide products and service to their
complete satisfaction. The foundation of this goal is to
build a total commitment towards quality. The Company
will also increasingly focus in high growth potential
segments like nutraceuticals and health products.
These new areas will add significant depth to the existing
product pipeline
